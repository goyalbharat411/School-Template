Our Vision

The vision of SRN International School to produce responsible young people,of whom all can be proud of. To encourage children to think independently, to be passionate about what they wish to do, to value excellence. With knowledge, confidence, compassion and cheer, children must proceed to make the world a better place than they found it.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop young men with creative and active minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others and the mettle to act on their beliefs. We emphasize on the holistic development of every child be it, spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional and physical. We aim to achieve excellence in learning both within the classroom and beyond, for which teachers are empowered to guide children with means to construct knowledge, this would nudge the child to grow into full bloom.
